# Story 6.4: Equipment Maintenance Scheduling + Alerts

## Status
Complete

## Story
**As a** clinic operations manager and maintenance technician,
**I want** automated equipment maintenance scheduling with proactive alerts and comprehensive tracking,
**so that** I can ensure equipment reliability, prevent downtime, and maintain compliance with medical device regulations.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated maintenance scheduling based on equipment specifications and usage patterns
2. Proactive maintenance alerts with <60s notification delivery for scheduled maintenance
3. Equipment lifecycle tracking with usage monitoring and performance analytics
4. Preventive maintenance calendar with automatic scheduling and resource allocation
5. Maintenance history tracking with complete documentation and compliance records
6. Equipment downtime monitoring with cost impact analysis and optimization
7. Vendor service integration for external maintenance and warranty management
8. Regulatory compliance tracking for medical device maintenance requirements
9. Maintenance cost tracking and budget planning with ROI analysis
10. Shadow testing for all maintenance calculations with accuracy validation

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-6-inventory-management.md] - **Equipment Maintenance**: Scheduling and alerts with cost optimization and regulatory compliance.

### Maintenance Requirements
- **Alert Speed**: <60s notification delivery for scheduled maintenance
- **Accuracy**: 100% reliable maintenance scheduling
- **Compliance**: Full regulatory compliance for medical devices
- **Cost Optimization**: Maintenance cost reduction through predictive scheduling

### Performance Requirements
- **Schedule Processing**: <5s for maintenance schedule generation
- **Alert Delivery**: <60s for maintenance notifications
- **Usage Tracking**: Real-time equipment usage monitoring
- **Report Generation**: <10s for maintenance analytics reports

### Database Schema Extensions
- equipment (id, name, model, serial_number, installation_date, warranty_expiry, maintenance_interval)
- maintenance_schedules (id, equipment_id, maintenance_type, scheduled_date, technician_id, status)
- maintenance_history (id, equipment_id, maintenance_date, maintenance_type, duration, cost, notes)
- equipment_usage (id, equipment_id, usage_hours, performance_metrics, recorded_date)

### Integration Points
- **Story 6.1**: Equipment inventory tracking
- **Story 3.1**: Patient treatment data for equipment usage
- **Story 2.1**: Appointment scheduling for maintenance planning
- **Financial System**: Cost tracking and budget management

### File Structure
- `lib/maintenance/scheduler.ts` - Automated maintenance scheduling
- `lib/maintenance/alert-system.ts` - Proactive maintenance alerts
- `lib/maintenance/lifecycle-tracker.ts` - Equipment lifecycle management
- `components/maintenance/` - Maintenance management UI components
- `app/api/maintenance/` - Maintenance management API endpoints
