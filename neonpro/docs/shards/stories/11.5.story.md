# Story 11.5: Predictive Maintenance System (Equipment Optimization)

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** clinic maintenance manager and operations director,
**I want** a predictive maintenance system for equipment optimization,
**so that** I can prevent equipment failures, reduce downtime, and ensure optimal equipment performance and safety.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Predictive maintenance system for clinic equipment optimization
2. Equipment performance monitoring and failure prediction algorithms
3. Automated maintenance scheduling based on usage patterns and predictions
4. Equipment lifecycle management and replacement planning
5. Maintenance cost optimization and budget planning
6. Integration with inventory management for spare parts and supplies
7. Compliance monitoring for medical equipment regulations and certifications
8. Performance analytics and maintenance effectiveness tracking
9. Emergency response system for critical equipment failures
10. Vendor integration for maintenance services and warranty management

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-11-predictive-analytics.md] - **Predictive Maintenance**: Equipment optimization and failure prevention.

### Equipment Categories
- **Medical Devices**: Treatment equipment, diagnostic tools, monitoring devices
- **Clinical Infrastructure**: HVAC, lighting, water systems, electrical systems
- **IT Equipment**: Computers, networking, software systems, security equipment
- **Facility Equipment**: Furniture, storage, cleaning, safety equipment

### Predictive Indicators
- **Usage Patterns**: Operating hours, frequency, intensity of use
- **Performance Metrics**: Efficiency, accuracy, speed, reliability
- **Environmental Factors**: Temperature, humidity, vibration, power quality
- **Historical Data**: Previous failures, maintenance records, replacement cycles

### Database Schema Extensions
- equipment_monitoring (id, equipment_id, metric_type, measurement_value, measurement_time, health_score)
- maintenance_predictions (id, equipment_id, predicted_failure_date, confidence_level, maintenance_urgency)
- maintenance_schedules (id, equipment_id, maintenance_type, scheduled_date, completion_status, cost)
- equipment_lifecycle (id, equipment_id, acquisition_date, expected_lifespan, replacement_recommendation)

### Integration Points
- **Story 6.1-6.4**: Inventory management for spare parts and supplies
- **Story 11.1**: Demand forecasting for maintenance resource planning
- **Story 4.1-4.5**: Financial intelligence for cost optimization
- **Story 12.1-12.5**: Compliance automation for regulatory requirements

### File Structure
- `lib/maintenance/predictive-system.ts` - Core predictive maintenance engine
- `lib/maintenance/equipment-monitoring.ts` - Equipment performance tracking
- `lib/maintenance/lifecycle-management.ts` - Equipment lifecycle and planning
- `components/maintenance/` - Maintenance management UI components
- `app/api/maintenance/` - Maintenance system API endpoints
