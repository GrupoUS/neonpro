# Story 9.4: Risk Assessment Automation with Medical Validation

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** medical practitioner and risk manager,
**I want** automated risk assessment with medical validation and human-in-the-loop oversight,
**so that** I can identify potential risks early, ensure patient safety, and maintain medical-grade decision support.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated risk assessment system with comprehensive patient risk analysis
2. Multi-factor risk evaluation including medical, procedural, and patient-specific risks
3. Real-time risk scoring during consultation and treatment planning
4. Human-in-the-loop medical validation for all high-risk assessments
5. Risk mitigation recommendations with evidence-based interventions
6. Integration with patient safety protocols and emergency procedures
7. Risk alert system with escalation procedures for critical risks
8. Audit trail for all risk assessments and medical validations
9. Risk prediction modeling for preventive care and early intervention
10. Compliance with medical standards and regulatory requirements for risk management

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-9-ai-treatment-engine.md] - **Risk Assessment Automation**: Medical validation with human-in-loop oversight for patient safety.

### Risk Categories
- **Medical Risks**: Conditions, allergies, contraindications
- **Procedural Risks**: Treatment-specific risks and complications
- **Patient-Specific Risks**: Age, health status, lifestyle factors
- **Environmental Risks**: Clinic conditions, equipment safety

### Validation Requirements
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: Medical professional oversight for high-risk cases
- **Medical Grade**: Hospital-grade safety and validation standards
- **Audit Trail**: Complete documentation of all assessments and decisions
- **Compliance**: Regulatory and medical standard adherence

### Database Schema Extensions
- risk_assessments (id, patient_id, risk_factors, risk_score, assessment_date, validation_status)
- risk_validations (id, assessment_id, validator_id, validation_decision, validation_notes, validation_date)
- risk_mitigations (id, risk_assessment_id, mitigation_strategy, implementation_status, effectiveness_score)
- risk_alerts (id, patient_id, risk_type, severity_level, alert_status, escalation_level)

### Integration Points
- **Patient Management**: Complete patient risk profile
- **Story 9.1**: Treatment predictions for risk assessment
- **Safety Protocols**: Emergency procedures and interventions
- **Medical Records**: Clinical data for risk evaluation

### File Structure
- `lib/risk/assessment-engine.ts` - Automated risk assessment

- `lib/risk/mitigation-system.ts` - Risk mitigation and safety
- `components/risk/` - Risk assessment UI components
