# Story 7.1: Patient Segmentation + AI-driven Insights

## Status
**Status:** COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** marketing manager and clinic administrator,
**I want** intelligent patient segmentation with AI-driven insights and ≥85% accuracy,
**so that** I can create targeted marketing campaigns, improve patient engagement, and optimize treatment recommendations.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. AI-powered patient segmentation with ≥85% accuracy for behavior prediction
2. Multi-dimensional segmentation criteria (demographics, behavior, treatment history, preferences)
3. Real-time segment updates based on patient interactions and treatment outcomes
4. Predictive analytics for patient lifetime value and treatment propensity
5. Automated segment creation based on predefined rules and AI recommendations
6. Segment performance tracking with engagement metrics and conversion rates
7. Custom segment builder with drag-and-drop interface for marketing teams
8. Integration with treatment recommendations and personalized care plans
9. LGPD-compliant patient data usage with consent management and privacy controls
10. Shadow testing for all segmentation algorithms with accuracy validation

### Performance Requirements
- **Segmentation Speed**: <5s for AI-powered segment generation

- segmentation_rules (id, rule_name, conditions, ai_recommendations, performance_metrics)
- segment_performance (id, segment_id, engagement_rate, conversion_rate, lifetime_value, analysis_date)
