# Story 13.2: Health Goal Management (Smart Goal Setting & Tracking)

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** patient care coordinator and health coach,
**I want** health goal management with smart goal setting and tracking,
**so that** I can create personalized health objectives, monitor patient progress, and adapt treatment plans based on wellness outcomes.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Smart health goal setting with AI-driven recommendations
2. Personalized goal templates based on patient conditions and treatment plans
3. Progress tracking with real-time wellness data integration
4. Goal achievement analytics and milestone recognition
5. Adaptive goal adjustment based on patient progress and medical changes
6. Multi-dimensional goal categories (fitness, nutrition, mental health, recovery)
7. Patient engagement tools with motivation and reminder systems
8. Care team collaboration for goal alignment and medical supervision
9. Integration with wearable device data for automatic progress updates
10. Comprehensive goal reporting and outcome analysis

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-13-wellness-integration.md] - **Health Goal Management**: Smart goal setting with progress tracking and care team collaboration.

### AI Goal Recommendation Engine
- **Condition-Based**: Goals aligned with specific medical conditions
- **Progress-Based**: Adaptive recommendations based on achievement patterns
- **Evidence-Based**: Goals based on clinical research and best practices
- **Personalized**: AI learning from patient preferences and behaviors

### Goal Categories
- **Fitness**: Exercise frequency, duration, intensity, type
- **Nutrition**: Caloric intake, macronutrient balance, meal timing
- **Mental Health**: Stress management, mindfulness practice, sleep quality
- **Recovery**: Physical therapy compliance, pain levels, mobility improvement
- **Preventive Care**: Screening schedules, medication adherence, checkup attendance

### Database Schema Extensions
- health_goals (id, patient_id, category, title, description, target_value, target_date, status, priority)
- goal_templates (id, condition_type, category, template_name, default_target, duration, difficulty)
- goal_progress (id, goal_id, recorded_date, current_value, progress_percentage, data_source)
- goal_milestones (id, goal_id, milestone_name, target_value, achieved_date, recognition_type)
- goal_adjustments (id, goal_id, adjustment_date, old_target, new_target, reason, adjusted_by)

### Integration Points
- **Story 13.1**: Wearable device data for automatic progress tracking
- **Story 13.3-13.5**: Wellness ecosystem integration
- **Patient Management**: Goal alignment with treatment plans
- **Story 9.1-9.5**: AI treatment engine for goal recommendations

### File Structure
- `lib/wellness/goal-management.ts` - Core goal management system
- `lib/wellness/goal-ai.ts` - AI-driven goal recommendations
- `lib/wellness/goal-tracking.ts` - Progress tracking and analytics
- `components/wellness/goals/` - Goal management UI components
- `app/api/wellness/goals/` - Goal management API endpoints
