## Story
**As a** wellness coordinator and patient care provider,
**I want** AI-powered lifestyle coaching with personalized wellness guidance,
**so that** I can provide tailored health recommendations, support patient behavior change, and improve long-term wellness outcomes.

4. Nutrition coaching with meal planning and dietary guidance

6. Stress management and mental health coaching with mindfulness techniques
7. Sleep optimization coaching with sleep hygiene recommendations
8. Habit formation support with tracking and reinforcement systems
9. Progress monitoring with adaptive coaching adjustments
10. Integration with care team for medical supervision and approval

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-13-wellness-integration.md] - **Lifestyle Coaching**: AI-powered wellness guidance with behavioral change support and care team integration.

### AI Coaching Algorithms
- **Recommendation Engine**: Collaborative filtering and content-based recommendations
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Pattern recognition and prediction models
- **Natural Language Processing**: Conversational coaching and motivational messaging
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Adaptive coaching strategy optimization

### Evidence-Based Coaching Techniques
- **Motivational Interviewing**: Patient-centered communication techniques
- **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy**: Thought pattern modification strategies
- **Transtheoretical Model**: Stages of change behavior modification
- **Social Cognitive Theory**: Self-efficacy and goal-setting frameworks

### Coaching Domains
- **Nutrition**: Meal planning, dietary guidelines, nutritional education
- **Exercise**: Workout plans, fitness goals, activity recommendations
- **Sleep**: Sleep hygiene, sleep environment optimization, sleep scheduling
- **Stress Management**: Mindfulness, relaxation techniques, stress reduction
- **Mental Health**: Mood tracking, coping strategies, emotional wellness

### Database Schema Extensions
- coaching_plans (id, patient_id, plan_type, objectives, strategies, start_date, end_date, status)
- coaching_sessions (id, plan_id, session_date, session_type, content, patient_response, effectiveness_score)
- lifestyle_assessments (id, patient_id, assessment_date, domain, scores, recommendations)
- coaching_recommendations (id, patient_id, domain, recommendation_text, evidence_level, implementation_status)
- behavior_tracking (id, patient_id, behavior_type, target_frequency, actual_frequency, tracking_date)

### Integration Points
- **Story 13.1**: Wearable data for coaching insights
- **Story 13.2**: Goal management integration with coaching plans
- **Story 13.3**: Dashboard integration for coaching progress
- **Patient Management**: Care team collaboration for coaching oversight
- **Story 9.1-9.5**: AI treatment engine for evidence-based recommendations

### File Structure
- `lib/wellness/coaching.ts` - Core lifestyle coaching system

- `lib/wellness/behavior-change.ts` - Behavioral change support system
- `components/wellness/coaching/` - Coaching interface UI components
- `app/api/wellness/coaching/` - Lifestyle coaching API endpoints
