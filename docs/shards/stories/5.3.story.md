# Story 5.3: Automated Invoice Generation + Payment Tracking

## Status
COMPLETE ✅ - Already implemented in Epic 4, Story 4.1

## Story
**As a** clinic administrator and billing manager,
**I want** automated invoice generation with comprehensive payment tracking and Brazilian compliance,
**so that** I can achieve ≥95% billing automation and eliminate manual invoice processing while ensuring regulatory compliance.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated invoice generation for all clinic services with Brazilian fiscal requirements
2. Real-time payment tracking across multiple payment methods and channels
3. Brazilian tax integration (NFSe generation, CNPJ validation, fiscal compliance)
4. Automated payment reminders and dunning process with escalation workflows
5. Invoice customization with clinic branding and professional formatting
6. Multi-currency support and tax calculation for international patients
7. Integration with insurance systems and health plan processing
8. Automated reconciliation between invoices and payments with matching algorithms
9. Comprehensive billing analytics and revenue tracking with KPI monitoring
10. Shadow testing for all billing calculations with 100% accuracy validation

### Performance Requirements
- **Invoice Generation**: <2s per invoice with batch processing capabilities
- **Payment Processing**: <1s for payment status updates

- **Report Generation**: <5s for billing analytics and reports

- payment_reminders (id, invoice_id, reminder_type, sent_date, response_status)
