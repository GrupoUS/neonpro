## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-16-advanced-ai-personalization.md] - **Treatment Outcome Optimization**: Automated enhancement achieving +25% improvement with continuous learning and research integration.

### Optimization Algorithms
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Q-learning, Policy Gradient for treatment protocol optimization
- **Multi-Objective Optimization**: Pareto optimization for competing treatment goals
- **Bayesian Optimization**: Efficient search for optimal treatment parameters
- **Genetic Algorithms**: Complex treatment protocol evolution and optimization
- **Monte Carlo Methods**: Uncertainty quantification in treatment outcomes

### Outcome Measures
- **Clinical Outcomes**: Symptom improvement, biomarker changes, functional status
- **Patient-Reported Outcomes**: Quality of life, pain scores, satisfaction ratings
- **Process Outcomes**: Treatment adherence, care coordination, safety metrics
- **Economic Outcomes**: Cost-effectiveness, resource utilization, length of stay
- **Long-term Outcomes**: Survival rates, recurrence, chronic disease progression

### Optimization Targets
- **Efficacy**: Maximum treatment effectiveness and symptom improvement
- **Safety**: Minimum adverse events and treatment-related complications
- **Efficiency**: Optimal resource utilization and treatment duration
- **Patient Experience**: Maximum satisfaction and quality of life improvement
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Best value and return on treatment investment

### Database Schema Extensions
- treatment_outcomes (id, patient_id, treatment_id, outcome_measures, measurement_date, follow_up_period)
- outcome_predictions (id, patient_id, treatment_id, predicted_outcome, confidence_interval, prediction_date)
- optimization_algorithms (id, algorithm_name, optimization_target, performance_metrics, last_updated)
- treatment_protocols (id, protocol_name, protocol_steps, evidence_level, optimization_score, version)
- outcome_analytics (id, treatment_type, patient_cohort, outcome_improvement, optimization_effectiveness)
