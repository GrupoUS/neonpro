# Story 3.1: 360° Patient Profile Implementation

## Status
In Progress - Photo Recognition System Implemented

## Story
**As a** clinic staff member,
**I want** a comprehensive 360° patient profile system with AI-enhanced insights and complete medical history tracking,
**so that** I can provide personalized care with full context and make informed treatment decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Complete patient profile creation with biometric data and photo management
2. AI-powered patient insights including risk assessment and treatment recommendations
3. Comprehensive medical history timeline with visual treatment progression
4. Automated duplicate patient detection with intelligent merge suggestions
5. Photo recognition system for patient identification and verification
6. Integration with existing appointment and treatment systems
7. Real-time patient data synchronization across all clinic touchpoints
8. LGPD-compliant data collection with automated consent management
9. Mobile-responsive interface for tablet and smartphone access
10. Advanced search capabilities with filters and patient segmentation

### AI/ML Requirements
- **Risk Assessment**: 80%+ accuracy for patient health risk evaluation
- **Duplicate Detection**: ≥95% accuracy for patient matching
- **Photo Recognition**: Facial recognition with privacy compliance
- **Pattern Analysis**: Behavioral and health trend analysis

### Performance Requirements
- **Profile Creation**: <30s for complete patient profile setup
- **Search Performance**: <2s response time for patient search
- **Photo Processing**: <5s for photo upload and recognition
- **AI Insights**: <3s for risk assessment and recommendations

### Database Schema Extensions
- patient_profiles_extended (patient_id, biometric_data_json, ai_insights_json, risk_score, last_assessment_date)
- patient_photos (id, patient_id, photo_url, photo_type, recognition_data_json, upload_date)
- medical_timeline (id, patient_id, event_type, event_date, description, photos, outcome_score)
- duplicate_candidates (id, patient_id_1, patient_id_2, confidence_score, review_status, resolved_date)

### Integration Points
- **Epic 1**: Patient profiles integrated with appointment booking
- **Epic 2**: Patient data used for intelligent scheduling
- **Financial System**: Patient profiles linked to billing and payment history
