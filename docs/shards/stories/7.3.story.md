# Story 7.3: Treatment Follow-up Automation

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** clinic administrator and healthcare provider,
**I want** automated treatment follow-up with intelligent scheduling and patient monitoring,
**so that** I can ensure optimal treatment outcomes, improve patient compliance, and enhance care quality.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated follow-up scheduling based on treatment protocols and patient needs
2. Intelligent follow-up timing optimization using patient behavior and response patterns
3. Multi-channel follow-up communication (SMS, WhatsApp, email, phone calls)
4. Treatment compliance monitoring with automated alerts for missed appointments
5. Personalized follow-up content based on treatment type and patient history
6. Automated outcome tracking and treatment effectiveness measurement
7. Integration with medical protocols and evidence-based follow-up guidelines
8. Escalation workflows for patients requiring additional attention or intervention
9. LGPD-compliant patient communication with consent management
10. Shadow testing for all follow-up algorithms with treatment outcome validation

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-7-crm-campaigns.md] - **Treatment Follow-up Automation**: Automated follow-ups with compliance and LGPD requirements.

### Follow-up Requirements
- **Automation**: Comprehensive follow-up automation based on treatment protocols
- **Intelligence**: AI-driven timing optimization and personalization
- **Compliance**: Treatment compliance monitoring and intervention
- **Medical Integration**: Evidence-based protocols and guidelines

### Performance Requirements
- **Scheduling Speed**: <3s for automated follow-up schedule generation
- **Communication Delivery**: <30s for multi-channel message delivery
- **Compliance Analysis**: <5s for compliance score calculation
- **Outcome Tracking**: Real-time outcome data processing and analysis

### Database Schema Extensions
- follow_up_schedules (id, patient_id, treatment_id, follow_up_type, scheduled_date, completion_status)
- treatment_protocols (id, treatment_type, follow_up_intervals, compliance_requirements, outcome_measures)
- follow_up_communications (id, schedule_id, channel, content, sent_date, response_status)
- treatment_outcomes (id, patient_id, treatment_id, outcome_measure, value, recorded_date)

- **Story 3.1**: Patient treatment data for follow-up planning
