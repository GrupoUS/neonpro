## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-15-iot-integration.md] - **Automated Calibration**: Equipment calibration automation with alerts and compliance tracking.

### Calibration Types
- **Periodic Calibration**: Time-based calibration schedules
- **Usage-Based**: Calibration triggered by equipment usage thresholds
- **Drift-Based**: Automatic calibration when drift exceeds tolerance
- **Pre-Use**: Calibration verification before critical procedures
- **Post-Maintenance**: Calibration after equipment service or repair

### Equipment Calibration Standards
- **Medical Devices**: FDA 21 CFR Part 820 quality system requirements
- **Laboratory Equipment**: ISO 17025 calibration and testing standards
- **Measurement Equipment**: NIST traceability and calibration guidelines
- **Imaging Equipment**: ACR and AAPM calibration protocols
- **Monitoring Devices**: IEC 60601 medical electrical equipment standards

### Calibration Parameters
- **Accuracy**: Deviation from known reference standards
- **Precision**: Repeatability and reproducibility of measurements
- **Linearity**: Response consistency across measurement range
- **Stability**: Measurement consistency over time
- **Drift**: Gradual change in calibration over time

### Database Schema Extensions
- calibration_schedules (id, equipment_id, calibration_type, frequency, next_due_date, assigned_technician)
- calibration_records (id, equipment_id, calibration_date, technician_id, results, compliance_status)
- calibration_standards (id, standard_name, reference_value, uncertainty, traceability_chain)
- calibration_alerts (id, equipment_id, alert_type, severity, generated_date, resolution_status)
- calibration_procedures (id, equipment_type, manufacturer, procedure_steps, required_standards)
