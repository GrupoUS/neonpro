# Story 9.2: Personalized Treatment Recommendations

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** medical practitioner,
**I want** AI-powered personalized treatment recommendations with ≥75% user acceptance,
**so that** I can provide customized treatment plans, improve patient satisfaction, and optimize treatment outcomes based on individual patient characteristics.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Personalized treatment recommendation engine based on individual patient profiles
2. Multi-dimensional analysis including medical history, preferences, and lifestyle factors
3. Treatment option ranking with success probability and risk assessment
4. Patient-specific customization with contraindications and allergies consideration
5. Integration with treatment protocols for personalized plan generation
6. Real-time recommendation updates based on patient response and progress
7. Alternative treatment suggestions with comparative analysis and rationale
8. Patient communication tools for recommendation explanation and consent
9. Medical professional approval workflow with override capabilities
10. User acceptance tracking to achieve ≥75% medical professional adoption

### Technical Architecture Context

### Personalization Factors
- **Medical History**: Past treatments, outcomes, conditions
- **Patient Preferences**: Treatment preferences, lifestyle factors
- **Demographics**: Age, gender, genetic factors
- **Safety Profile**: Allergies, contraindications, risk factors

### Recommendation Categories
- **Primary Treatments**: Most suitable options with highest success probability
- **Alternative Options**: Secondary choices with comparative analysis
- **Combination Therapies**: Multi-treatment approaches for complex cases
- **Preventive Measures**: Risk reduction and maintenance recommendations

### Database Schema Extensions
- recommendation_profiles (id, patient_id, profile_data, preference_weights, last_updated)
- treatment_recommendations (id, patient_id, treatment_options, ranking_scores, rationale)
- recommendation_feedback (id, recommendation_id, provider_id, feedback_type, adoption_status)
- personalization_factors (id, factor_type, factor_value, weight, patient_id, source)

- **Story 9.1**: Treatment prediction for success probability
