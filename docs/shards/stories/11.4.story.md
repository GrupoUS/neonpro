# Story 11.4: Automated Scheduling Optimization (Efficiency Maximization)

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** clinic operations coordinator and scheduling manager,
**I want** automated scheduling optimization for efficiency maximization,
**so that** I can optimize resource utilization, reduce scheduling conflicts, and maximize clinic operational efficiency.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated scheduling optimization system for maximum efficiency
2. Real-time schedule optimization based on demand forecasting and no-show predictions
3. Resource allocation optimization for staff, equipment, and facilities
4. Intelligent appointment slot allocation with efficiency prioritization
5. Automated rescheduling and slot reallocation for optimal utilization
6. Multi-constraint optimization considering patient preferences and clinic capacity
7. Performance monitoring and efficiency metrics tracking
8. Integration with demand forecasting and no-show prediction systems
9. Scheduling conflict resolution and automated adjustment capabilities
10. Optimization reporting and analytics for continuous improvement

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-11-predictive-analytics.md] - **Automated Scheduling Optimization**: Efficiency maximization.

### Optimization Components
- **Algorithm Engine**: Multi-constraint optimization algorithms
- **Real-time Integration**: Dynamic optimization with live data
- **Resource Management**: Staff, equipment, and facility optimization
- **Efficiency Metrics**: Performance tracking and improvement

### Optimization Constraints
- **Patient Preferences**: Preferred times, staff, and service types
- **Resource Availability**: Staff schedules, equipment, and room capacity
- **Service Requirements**: Duration, preparation time, and equipment needs
- **Clinic Policies**: Operating hours, break times, and scheduling rules

### Database Schema Extensions
- schedule_optimizations (id, optimization_date, efficiency_score, conflicts_resolved, utilization_improvement)
- optimization_constraints (id, constraint_type, constraint_value, priority_level, enforcement_rule)
- efficiency_metrics (id, measurement_date, utilization_rate, conflict_count, optimization_impact)
- automated_adjustments (id, original_schedule, optimized_schedule, adjustment_reason, efficiency_gain)
