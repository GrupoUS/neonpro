# Story 16.1: AI Personalization Engine (Individual Patient Treatment Optimization)

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** healthcare provider and treatment specialist,
**I want** an AI personalization engine for individual patient treatment optimization,
**so that** I can deliver highly personalized care with ≥90% personalization accuracy and achieve +25% improvement in treatment outcomes.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. AI personalization engine with ≥90% accuracy for individual patient optimization
2. Comprehensive patient data analysis including medical history, genetics, and lifestyle
3. Personalized treatment plan generation based on multi-dimensional patient profiles
4. Real-time adaptation of treatment plans based on patient response and outcomes
5. Machine learning models for continuous improvement of personalization algorithms
6. Integration with clinical decision support systems and evidence-based protocols
7. Patient preference learning and incorporation into treatment personalization
8. Risk stratification and personalized safety protocols for individual patients
9. Outcome prediction and treatment success probability modeling
10. Clinical validation and approval workflow for AI-generated recommendations

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-16-advanced-ai-personalization.md] - **AI Personalization Engine**: Individual patient optimization with ≥90% accuracy and +25% outcome improvement.

### AI/ML Technologies
- **Deep Learning**: Neural networks for complex pattern recognition
- **Ensemble Methods**: Random Forest, Gradient Boosting for robust predictions
- **Natural Language Processing**: Clinical note analysis and information extraction
- **Computer Vision**: Medical imaging analysis and feature extraction
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Treatment protocol optimization through trial and learning

### Personalization Dimensions
- **Clinical**: Diagnosis, comorbidities, medication history, allergies
- **Genetic**: Pharmacogenomics, disease susceptibility, treatment response
- **Lifestyle**: Diet, exercise, sleep patterns, stress levels
- **Social**: Cultural background, health beliefs, support systems
- **Behavioral**: Adherence patterns, health engagement, lifestyle choices

### Machine Learning Pipeline
- **Data Collection**: Multi-source patient data aggregation
- **Feature Engineering**: Clinical variable extraction and transformation
- **Model Training**: Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms
- **Validation**: Cross-validation and clinical outcome correlation
- **Deployment**: Real-time inference and recommendation generation
- **Monitoring**: Performance tracking and model drift detection

### Database Schema Extensions
- patient_profiles (id, patient_id, profile_data, last_updated, profile_version, personalization_score)
- ai_models (id, model_name, model_type, accuracy_score, training_date, deployment_status)
- personalization_recommendations (id, patient_id, model_id, recommendation_data, confidence_score, created_date)
- treatment_outcomes (id, patient_id, treatment_id, outcome_measures, follow_up_date, improvement_score)
- preference_profiles (id, patient_id, preference_type, preference_value, weight, last_updated)
