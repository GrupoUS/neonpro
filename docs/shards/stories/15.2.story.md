7. Cost analysis and ROI tracking for equipment investments and maintenance
8. Integration with manufacturer maintenance protocols and recommendations
9. Alert system for equipment failures, maintenance needs, and performance issues
10. Compliance tracking for regulatory requirements and safety standards

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-15-iot-integration.md] - **Equipment Monitoring**: Usage tracking with ≥80% maintenance prediction accuracy and +15% uptime improvement.

### Predictive Maintenance Algorithms
- **Time Series Analysis**: ARIMA, seasonal decomposition for usage pattern prediction
- **Machine Learning**: Random Forest, SVM for failure prediction and classification
- **Anomaly Detection**: Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM for abnormal behavior detection
- **Deep Learning**: LSTM networks for complex temporal pattern recognition

### Equipment Metrics Tracking
- **Usage Metrics**: Operating hours, cycle counts, patient procedures performed
- **Performance Metrics**: Accuracy, precision, calibration drift, response times
- **Health Metrics**: Temperature, vibration, power consumption, error rates
- **Maintenance Metrics**: Time between failures, repair times, parts replacement

### Maintenance Categories
- **Preventive**: Scheduled maintenance based on time or usage thresholds
- **Predictive**: AI-driven maintenance based on condition monitoring
- **Corrective**: Reactive maintenance following equipment failures
- **Emergency**: Immediate response for critical equipment failures

### Database Schema Extensions
- equipment_usage (id, equipment_id, usage_start, usage_end, procedure_type, user_id, usage_duration)
- equipment_metrics (id, equipment_id, metric_type, metric_value, recorded_timestamp, quality_score)
- maintenance_predictions (id, equipment_id, prediction_type, predicted_date, confidence_score, model_version)
- maintenance_schedules (id, equipment_id, maintenance_type, scheduled_date, assigned_technician, status)
- equipment_health (id, equipment_id, health_score, performance_index, risk_level, assessment_date)

### Integration Points
- **Story 15.1**: IoT gateway for real-time equipment data collection
- **Story 15.3**: Environmental factors in equipment performance
- **Story 15.4**: Automated calibration integration
- **Story 15.5**: Analytics for equipment optimization
- **Story 6.1-6.5**: Inventory management for maintenance parts
