# Story 10.4: Healthcare Compliance Computer Vision (Medical Device Standards)

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** medical device compliance officer and quality assurance manager,
**I want** healthcare compliance computer vision adhering to medical device standards,
**so that** I can ensure regulatory compliance, maintain quality standards, and meet healthcare certification requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Medical device standards compliance for computer vision systems (FDA, CE, ANVISA)
2. Healthcare-grade accuracy and validation requirements for vision analysis
3. Patient privacy and data protection compliance for medical imaging
4. Clinical workflow integration with regulatory documentation requirements
5. Quality assurance and validation protocols for medical computer vision
6. Audit trails and compliance reporting for regulatory submissions
7. Risk management and safety protocols for medical device operation
8. Interoperability standards compliance for healthcare system integration
9. Performance monitoring and quality metrics for regulatory compliance
10. Continuous compliance monitoring and regulatory update management

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-10-computer-vision.md] - **Healthcare Compliance**: Medical device standards with regulatory compliance.

### Regulatory Frameworks
- **FDA**: Food and Drug Administration (US) medical device regulations
- **CE**: European Conformity marking for medical devices
- **ANVISA**: Brazilian Health Regulatory Agency compliance
- **ISO 14971**: Medical device risk management standards
- **ISO 13485**: Medical device quality management systems
- **IEC 62304**: Medical device software lifecycle processes

### Privacy & Security Standards
- **HIPAA**: US healthcare data privacy requirements
- **LGPD**: Brazilian General Data Protection Law
- **GDPR**: European General Data Protection Regulation
- **Data Security**: Medical-grade encryption and access controls

### Database Schema Extensions
- compliance_validations (id, system_component, standard_type, validation_status, compliance_date)
- regulatory_audits (id, audit_type, audit_date, auditor, compliance_score, findings)
- quality_metrics (id, metric_type, measurement_date, value, threshold, compliance_status)
- regulatory_submissions (id, submission_type, agency, submission_date, approval_status, reference_number)

### Integration Points
- **All Vision Stories**: Compliance framework for all computer vision features
- **Patient Management**: Privacy-compliant patient data handling
- **Quality Assurance**: Medical-grade validation and testing
- **Audit Systems**: Regulatory compliance documentation and reporting

### File Structure
- `lib/compliance/` - Medical device compliance framework
- `lib/compliance/regulatory-standards.ts` - FDA, CE, ANVISA compliance
- `lib/compliance/privacy-protection.ts` - HIPAA, LGPD, GDPR compliance
- `components/compliance/` - Compliance monitoring UI components
- `app/api/compliance/` - Regulatory compliance API endpoints
