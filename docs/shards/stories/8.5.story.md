# Story 8.5: ROI Analysis for Marketing + Treatments

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** clinic owner and marketing manager,
**I want** comprehensive ROI analysis for marketing campaigns and treatments,
**so that** I can optimize investment decisions, maximize profitability, and allocate resources to highest-performing activities.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Comprehensive ROI calculation for all marketing campaigns and channels
2. Treatment-specific ROI analysis with profitability optimization
3. Patient acquisition cost (CAC) calculation and lifetime value (LTV) analysis
4. Marketing attribution tracking across multiple touchpoints and channels
5. Comparative ROI analysis between different marketing strategies and treatments
6. Real-time ROI monitoring with automated alerts for underperforming investments
7. Predictive ROI modeling for future campaigns and treatment planning
8. Cost optimization recommendations based on ROI analysis
9. Integration with financial and patient systems for accurate ROI calculation
10. Executive ROI dashboard with actionable insights and strategic recommendations

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-8-advanced-bi.md] - **ROI Analysis**: Comprehensive marketing and treatment ROI with optimization recommendations.

### ROI Categories
- **Marketing ROI**: Campaign performance, channel effectiveness, conversion rates
- **Treatment ROI**: Service profitability, resource utilization, pricing optimization
- **Patient Value**: Acquisition cost, lifetime value, retention impact
- **Strategic ROI**: Long-term investment analysis, growth planning

### Financial Integration
- **Cost Tracking**: Marketing spend, treatment costs, operational expenses
- **Revenue Attribution**: Patient revenue by acquisition channel and treatment
- **Profitability Analysis**: Margin calculation, ROI optimization
- **Budget Planning**: ROI-based budget allocation and forecasting

### Database Schema Extensions
- marketing_roi (id, campaign_id, total_spend, revenue_attributed, patients_acquired, roi_percentage)
- treatment_profitability (id, treatment_type, treatment_cost, average_revenue, profit_margin, roi_score)
- patient_acquisition (id, patient_id, acquisition_channel, acquisition_cost, lifetime_value, payback_period)
- roi_forecasts (id, investment_type, predicted_roi, confidence_interval, forecast_period)

### Integration Points
- **Financial System**: Cost and revenue data for ROI calculation
- **Marketing System**: Campaign data and attribution tracking
- **Story 8.1**: Dashboard integration for ROI visualization
- **Patient Management**: Patient value and retention data

### File Structure
- `lib/roi/calculation-engine.ts` - ROI calculation and analysis
- `lib/roi/attribution-tracker.ts` - Marketing attribution tracking
- `lib/roi/optimization-engine.ts` - ROI optimization recommendations
- `components/roi/` - ROI analysis UI components
- `app/api/roi/` - ROI analysis API endpoints
