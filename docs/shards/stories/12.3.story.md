# Story 12.3: CFM Requirements Validation (Medical Procedures)

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** medical director and compliance manager,
**I want** CFM requirements validation for medical procedures,
**so that** I can ensure compliance with Federal Council of Medicine regulations, maintain professional standards, and validate medical practice requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. CFM requirements validation system for medical procedures
2. Medical professional certification and credential validation
3. Procedure authorization and supervision requirement validation
4. Medical ethics and standards compliance monitoring
5. Professional responsibility and liability tracking
6. Continuing medical education (CME) compliance management
7. Medical procedure documentation and evidence requirements
8. Informed consent validation for medical procedures
9. Medical emergency and safety protocol compliance
10. CFM regulatory reporting and compliance documentation

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-12-compliance-automation.md] - **CFM Requirements Validation**: Medical procedures compliance.

### CFM Regulatory Areas
- **Professional Practice**: Medical license and certification requirements
- **Procedure Authorization**: Medical procedure approval and supervision
- **Medical Ethics**: Ethical standards and professional conduct
- **Patient Safety**: Safety protocols and emergency procedures
- **Documentation**: Medical record and evidence requirements

### Validation Categories
- **Physician Credentials**: License, specialty certification, CME compliance
- **Procedure Approval**: Authorization levels and supervision requirements
- **Patient Consent**: Informed consent and communication requirements
- **Safety Compliance**: Emergency protocols and safety standards

### Database Schema Extensions
- cfm_validations (id, validation_type, procedure_id, physician_id, compliance_status, validation_date)
- professional_credentials (id, physician_id, credential_type, issue_date, expiry_date, status, cme_hours)
- procedure_authorizations (id, procedure_id, physician_id, authorization_level, supervisor_id, approval_status)
- medical_incidents (id, incident_type, procedure_id, physician_id, incident_date, resolution_status, cfm_reported)

### Integration Points
- **Staff Management**: Physician credentials and certification tracking
- **Procedure Management**: Medical procedure authorization and validation
- **Patient Care**: Informed consent and patient communication
- **Quality Assurance**: Medical safety and compliance monitoring

### File Structure
- `lib/compliance/cfm-validation.ts` - Core CFM requirements validation
- `lib/compliance/professional-credentials.ts` - Physician credential management
- `lib/compliance/procedure-authorization.ts` - Procedure approval and supervision
- `components/compliance/cfm/` - CFM compliance UI components
- `app/api/compliance/cfm/` - CFM validation API endpoints
