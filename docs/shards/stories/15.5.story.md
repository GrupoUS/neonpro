## Story

**I want** comprehensive equipment optimization analytics with usage analysis and performance optimization,
**so that** I can maximize equipment efficiency, reduce operational costs, and make data-driven decisions for equipment investments and resource allocation.

7. Predictive analytics for equipment replacement and upgrade planning
8. Energy efficiency analysis with environmental impact and cost optimization
9. Integration with manufacturer APIs for benchmarking and best practices
10. Executive dashboard with KPIs, trends, and actionable insights

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-15-iot-integration.md] - **Equipment Optimization Analytics**: Usage analytics with performance optimization and cost-benefit analysis.

### Analytics Categories
- **Utilization Analytics**: Equipment usage rates, idle time, peak usage patterns
- **Performance Analytics**: Efficiency metrics, throughput analysis, quality indicators
- **Financial Analytics**: ROI, TCO, operational costs, investment analysis
- **Predictive Analytics**: Failure prediction, replacement planning, demand forecasting
- **Comparative Analytics**: Benchmarking, best practices, market analysis

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Utilization Rate**: Percentage of available time equipment is in use
- **Efficiency Score**: Ratio of productive time to total operational time
- **Cost per Procedure**: Total operational cost divided by procedures performed
- **ROI Percentage**: Return on investment for equipment purchases
- **Energy Efficiency**: Energy consumption per unit of output

### Optimization Algorithms
- **Linear Programming**: Resource allocation and scheduling optimization
- **Machine Learning**: Pattern recognition and predictive modeling
- **Statistical Analysis**: Trend analysis and correlation identification
- **Simulation Modeling**: Scenario planning and capacity analysis
- **Genetic Algorithms**: Complex optimization problem solving

### Database Schema Extensions
- equipment_analytics (id, equipment_id, analysis_period, utilization_rate, efficiency_score, cost_metrics)
- optimization_recommendations (id, equipment_id, recommendation_type, description, potential_savings, implementation_status)
- financial_metrics (id, equipment_id, period, revenue_generated, operational_costs, roi_percentage)
- comparative_benchmarks (id, equipment_type, benchmark_metric, industry_average, clinic_performance, variance)
- predictive_models (id, model_type, equipment_category, prediction_accuracy, last_updated, model_parameters)

### Integration Points
- **Story 15.1**: IoT gateway for real-time equipment data
- **Story 15.2**: Equipment monitoring for performance tracking
- **Story 15.3**: Environmental impact on equipment efficiency
- **Story 15.4**: Calibration impact on equipment performance
- **Story 8.1-8.5**: Business intelligence integration for analytics

### File Structure
- `lib/iot/analytics.ts` - Equipment optimization analytics core

- `lib/iot/financial-analytics.ts` - Financial analysis and ROI tracking
- `components/iot/analytics/` - Analytics dashboard UI components
- `app/api/iot/analytics/` - Equipment analytics API endpoints
