# Story 2.2: Intelligent Conflict Resolution

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** clinic receptionist,
**I want** an intelligent system that automatically detects and resolves scheduling conflicts with smart suggestions,
**so that** I can efficiently manage bookings without manual conflict checking and provide optimal alternatives.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. System detects all types of scheduling conflicts (time, staff, room, equipment) in real-time
2. Automated resolution suggestions with multiple viable alternatives presented
3. Staff availability optimization considers skills, preferences, and workload balance
4. Room and equipment scheduling integrated with appointment conflict detection
5. Business rules enforcement (minimum breaks, maximum hours, holiday restrictions)
6. Intelligent rebooking suggestions based on patient preferences and history
7. Conflict resolution completes within <500ms for real-time booking experience
8. Priority-based conflict resolution (VIP patients, urgent treatments, etc.)
9. Automated waitlist management with smart notification timing
10. Integration with existing calendar views and appointment CRUD operations
