# Story 16.3: Behavior Prediction (Advanced Patient Behavior Analytics)

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** behavioral health specialist and care coordinator,
**I want** advanced patient behavior prediction with analytics and intervention recommendations,
**so that** I can proactively identify at-risk behaviors, predict patient actions, and intervene with personalized strategies to improve adherence and outcomes.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Advanced patient behavior prediction with machine learning algorithms
2. Adherence prediction models for medication and treatment compliance
3. Risk behavior identification with early intervention triggers
4. Personalized intervention recommendations based on behavior patterns
5. Real-time behavior monitoring with continuous learning and adaptation
6. Behavioral health integration with mental health and wellness tracking
7. Social determinant analysis and behavioral risk factor identification
8. Predictive modeling for appointment attendance and engagement
9. Behavioral intervention effectiveness tracking and optimization
10. Integration with care team workflows and patient engagement systems

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-16-advanced-ai-personalization.md] - **Behavior Prediction**: Advanced patient behavior analytics with intervention recommendations and care team integration.

### Behavior Prediction Models
- **Time Series Analysis**: LSTM, ARIMA for temporal behavior patterns
- **Classification Models**: Random Forest, SVM for behavior categorization
- **Deep Learning**: Neural networks for complex behavior pattern recognition
- **Survival Analysis**: Cox models for time-to-event behavior prediction
- **Clustering**: K-means, hierarchical clustering for behavior segmentation

### Behavioral Data Sources
- **Electronic Health Records**: Clinical visits, diagnoses, treatments
- **Patient Portal Usage**: Login patterns, message frequency, feature usage
- **Wearable Devices**: Activity levels, sleep patterns, heart rate variability
- **Medication Adherence**: Pharmacy refills, smart pill bottle data
- **Social Determinants**: Housing, employment, education, support systems

### Prediction Categories
- **Adherence**: Medication compliance, appointment attendance, therapy participation
- **Risk Behaviors**: Substance abuse, self-harm, treatment dropout
- **Engagement**: Portal usage, care plan participation, communication frequency
- **Health Behaviors**: Exercise, diet, sleep hygiene, stress management
- **Clinical Outcomes**: Symptom progression, recovery trajectory, readmission risk

### Database Schema Extensions
- behavior_predictions (id, patient_id, prediction_type, predicted_behavior, confidence_score, prediction_date)
- behavior_patterns (id, patient_id, pattern_type, pattern_data, identified_date, risk_level)
- intervention_recommendations (id, patient_id, behavior_type, intervention_strategy, evidence_level, created_date)
- behavior_monitoring (id, patient_id, behavior_metric, metric_value, recorded_timestamp, data_source)
- behavioral_interventions (id, patient_id, intervention_id, implementation_date, effectiveness_score, outcome)
