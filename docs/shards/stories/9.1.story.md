# Story 9.1: Treatment Success Prediction (≥85% Accuracy)

## Status
Done

## Story
**As a** medical practitioner and clinic manager,
**I want** AI-powered treatment success prediction with ≥85% accuracy and +20% treatment success improvement,
**so that** I can make informed treatment decisions, improve patient outcomes, and reduce failed treatments.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Machine learning models for treatment success prediction with ≥85% accuracy requirement
2. Multi-factor analysis including patient history, demographics, and treatment characteristics
3. Real-time prediction scoring during treatment selection and planning
4. Historical outcome validation and continuous model improvement
5. Integration with patient records for comprehensive prediction accuracy
6. Medical-grade validation with human-in-the-loop oversight
7. Prediction confidence intervals with risk assessment and uncertainty quantification
8. Treatment success improvement tracking with +20% target achievement
9. Model explainability for medical decision support and transparency
10. Performance monitoring with accuracy alerts and model retraining automation
