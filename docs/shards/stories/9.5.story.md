# Story 9.5: Medical Knowledge Base Integration

## Status
Done

## Story
**As a** medical practitioner and AI system user,
**I want** comprehensive medical knowledge base integration with research database access,
**so that** I can access current medical knowledge, validate AI recommendations, and ensure evidence-based treatment decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Integration with comprehensive medical knowledge databases and research repositories
2. Real-time access to current medical literature and treatment guidelines
3. Evidence-based validation for all AI recommendations and treatment suggestions
4. Medical research search and discovery tools for practitioners
5. Automatic updates from medical databases to keep knowledge current
6. Drug interaction checking with comprehensive pharmaceutical databases
7. Disease and condition reference system with current diagnostic criteria
8. Treatment guideline integration with protocol recommendations
9. Medical education and training resources for continuous learning
10. Knowledge base search optimization with medical terminology and semantic search

### Technical Architecture Context

### Knowledge Sources
- **Medical Literature**: PubMed, medical journals, research databases
- **Treatment Guidelines**: Medical society guidelines and protocols
- **Drug Databases**: Pharmaceutical information and interactions
- **Diagnostic Systems**: ICD, medical classification systems

### Integration Requirements
- **Real-time Access**: Live connection to medical databases
- **Semantic Search**: Medical terminology and concept recognition
- **Evidence Validation**: Research-backed recommendation verification
- **Continuous Updates**: Automatic knowledge base synchronization

### Database Schema Extensions
- knowledge_sources (id, source_name, source_type, api_endpoint, last_sync, status)
- medical_knowledge (id, knowledge_type, content_data, source_id, validity_date, evidence_level)
- research_cache (id, search_query, search_results, cache_date, relevance_score)
- validation_results (id, recommendation_id, knowledge_source, evidence_level, validation_status)

### Integration Points
- **Story 9.1**: Treatment predictions with evidence validation
- **Story 9.2**: Personalized recommendations with research backing
- **Story 9.3**: Protocol optimization with literature evidence
- **Story 9.4**: Risk assessment with medical knowledge validation
