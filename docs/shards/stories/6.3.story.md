# Story 6.3: Supplier Management + Performance Tracking

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** procurement manager and clinic administrator,
**I want** comprehensive supplier management with performance tracking and automated evaluation,
**so that** I can optimize supplier relationships, ensure reliable supply chains, and achieve 15% cost reduction through better vendor management.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Comprehensive supplier database with complete vendor information and documentation
2. Automated supplier performance tracking with KPI monitoring and scoring
3. Supplier evaluation system with rating algorithms and benchmarking
4. Contract management with renewal alerts and pricing optimization
5. Multi-supplier comparison and competitive bidding platform
6. Supplier communication portal with order tracking and issue resolution
7. Quality assurance tracking with defect rates and complaint management
8. Payment terms optimization and early payment discount management
9. Supplier risk assessment and contingency planning for critical supplies
10. Shadow testing for all supplier performance calculations with audit validation

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-6-inventory-management.md] - **Supplier Management**: Performance tracking with cost optimization targeting 15% margin improvement.

### Performance Tracking KPIs
- **Delivery Performance**: On-time delivery rate, lead time accuracy
- **Quality Metrics**: Defect rate, return rate, quality score
- **Pricing Competitiveness**: Price variance, cost savings, discount utilization
- **Service Quality**: Response time, issue resolution, communication effectiveness

### Cost Optimization Requirements
- **Target Savings**: 15% cost reduction through better vendor management
- **Pricing Analysis**: Competitive pricing tracking and negotiation support
- **Bulk Ordering**: Volume discount optimization and planning
- **Payment Terms**: Early payment discount maximization

### Database Schema Extensions
- suppliers (id, name, contact_info, contract_terms, performance_score, risk_level, status)
- supplier_contracts (id, supplier_id, contract_type, start_date, end_date, terms, renewal_alerts)
- supplier_performance (id, supplier_id, period, delivery_score, quality_score, price_score, service_score)
- purchase_orders (id, supplier_id, order_items, delivery_date, quality_rating, total_cost)

### Integration Points
- **Story 6.2**: Automated purchase order generation
- **Story 6.1**: Inventory tracking for supplier performance evaluation
- **Financial System**: Payment processing and cost tracking
- **Quality System**: Defect tracking and quality assurance

### File Structure
- `lib/suppliers/supplier-manager.ts` - Supplier management core
- `lib/suppliers/performance-tracker.ts` - Performance tracking and evaluation
- `lib/suppliers/bidding-platform.ts` - Competitive bidding system
- `components/suppliers/` - Supplier management UI components
- `app/api/suppliers/` - Supplier management API endpoints
