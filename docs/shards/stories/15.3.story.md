## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-15-iot-integration.md] - **Environmental Monitoring**: Temperature, humidity, and air quality with real-time control and compliance tracking.

### Environmental Sensors
- **Temperature**: High-precision thermistors and RTD sensors
- **Humidity**: Capacitive and resistive humidity sensors
- **Air Quality**: CO2, VOC, particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10) sensors
- **Pressure**: Barometric and differential pressure sensors
- **Light**: UV and illuminance sensors for patient comfort

### Healthcare Environmental Standards
- **ASHRAE**: HVAC standards for healthcare facilities
- **CDC Guidelines**: Infection control environmental requirements
- **Joint Commission**: Safety and environment of care standards
- **ISO 14644**: Cleanroom and controlled environment standards
- **OSHA**: Occupational safety and health environmental requirements

### Zone Classifications
- **Critical Care**: Operating rooms, ICU, emergency departments
- **Patient Care**: Patient rooms, examination rooms, treatment areas
- **Laboratory**: Clinical labs, pathology, specimen processing
- **Support Areas**: Storage, administrative, waiting areas
- **Specialty**: Imaging, pharmacy, sterilization areas

### Database Schema Extensions
- environmental_sensors (id, sensor_id, sensor_type, location_zone, calibration_date, status)
- environmental_data (id, sensor_id, measurement_type, value, unit, timestamp, quality_flag)
- environmental_zones (id, zone_name, zone_type, temperature_range, humidity_range, air_quality_standards)
- environmental_alerts (id, zone_id, alert_type, threshold_value, actual_value, alert_timestamp)
- hvac_controls (id, zone_id, control_action, setpoint_value, execution_timestamp, energy_impact)

### Integration Points
- **Story 15.1**: IoT gateway for sensor connectivity
- **Story 15.2**: Equipment performance correlation with environmental factors
- **Story 15.4**: Calibration environmental requirements
- **Story 12.1-12.5**: Compliance automation for regulatory requirements
- **Building Management**: HVAC and facility system integration
