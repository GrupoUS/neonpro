# Story 12.2: LGPD Consent Management Automation + Tracking

## Status
COMPLETED - BMAD Implementation

## Story
**As a** data protection officer and privacy compliance manager,
**I want** LGPD consent management automation with comprehensive tracking,
**so that** I can ensure data privacy compliance, manage patient consent effectively, and maintain complete audit trails for data processing activities.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. LGPD consent management automation with comprehensive tracking
2. Dynamic consent collection and management for all data processing activities
3. Automated consent validation and renewal management
4. Real-time consent status monitoring and compliance tracking
5. Patient rights management including access, rectification, and deletion
6. Data processing purpose tracking and consent mapping
7. Automated privacy impact assessments and risk management
8. Consent withdrawal management and data processing cessation
9. Privacy policy automation and consent form generation
10. LGPD compliance reporting and audit trail maintenance

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-12-compliance-automation.md] - **LGPD Consent Management**: Automation with comprehensive tracking.

### LGPD Requirements
- **Consent Collection**: Clear, informed, and specific consent
- **Data Subject Rights**: Access, rectification, deletion, portability
- **Lawful Basis**: Legal basis for data processing activities
- **Privacy by Design**: Built-in privacy protection and compliance
- **Data Protection Officer**: DPO responsibilities and oversight

### Consent Categories
- **Treatment Consent**: Medical treatment and care consent
- **Marketing Consent**: Marketing and promotional communications
- **Research Consent**: Clinical research and data analysis
- **Third-Party Sharing**: Data sharing with partners or providers

### Database Schema Extensions
- lgpd_consents (id, patient_id, consent_type, purpose, status, grant_date, expiry_date, withdrawal_date)
- data_processing_activities (id, activity_type, purpose, lawful_basis, consent_required, data_categories)
- patient_rights_requests (id, patient_id, request_type, request_date, status, completion_date, response_data)
- privacy_assessments (id, assessment_type, risk_level, mitigation_measures, assessment_date, review_date)

### Integration Points
- **Patient Management**: Patient data and treatment consent
- **Marketing Systems**: Marketing consent and communication preferences
- **Research Systems**: Research consent and data analysis
- **Audit Systems**: Comprehensive audit trail and compliance monitoring

### File Structure
- `lib/compliance/lgpd-consent.ts` - Core LGPD consent management
- `lib/compliance/patient-rights.ts` - Patient rights and request processing
- `lib/compliance/privacy-assessment.ts` - Privacy impact assessments
- `components/compliance/lgpd/` - LGPD compliance UI components
- `app/api/compliance/lgpd/` - LGPD compliance API endpoints
