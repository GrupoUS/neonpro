# Story 3.2: AI-powered Risk Assessment + Insights

## Status
COMPLETED

## Story
**As a** healthcare provider,
**I want** an AI system that automatically assesses patient risk factors and provides intelligent treatment insights,
**so that** I can make informed medical decisions and provide proactive preventive care.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Automated patient risk assessment with 80%+ accuracy for health predictions
2. AI-driven treatment recommendation engine based on patient history and outcomes
3. Predictive analytics for treatment success rates and potential complications
4. Intelligent patient behavior pattern analysis for personalized care plans
5. Real-time health trend monitoring with automated alerts for concerning patterns
6. Integration with medical databases for evidence-based recommendations
7. Personalized care pathway suggestions based on patient profile and treatment goals
8. Automated clinical decision support with safety alerts and contraindications
9. Patient outcome prediction modeling for treatment planning optimization
10. Continuous learning system that improves recommendations based on clinic outcomes

## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-3-smart-patient-management.md] - **AI-powered Risk Assessment**: 80%+ accuracy for patient health risk assessment with intelligent treatment recommendations.

[Source: architecture/ai-ml-integration.md] - **AI/ML Infrastructure**: Utilize established AI framework for patient analysis and predictive modeling.

### AI/ML Requirements
- **Models**: Risk assessment, treatment recommendation, outcome prediction
- **Accuracy Target**: 80%+ for health risk predictions
- **Learning**: Continuous improvement from clinic-specific outcomes
- **Safety**: Medical safety validation and contraindication detection

### Performance Requirements
- **Risk Assessment**: <3s for complete patient risk analysis
- **Recommendations**: <2s for treatment suggestions
- **Trend Analysis**: Real-time processing of health data updates
- **Prediction Accuracy**: Continuously monitored and improved

### Database Schema Extensions
- ai_risk_assessments (patient_id, assessment_date, risk_factors_json, overall_score, predictions_json)
- treatment_recommendations (id, patient_id, recommended_treatments_json, success_probability, evidence_level)
- prediction_models (model_id, model_type, training_date, accuracy_metrics, active_status)
- patient_outcomes (patient_id, treatment_id, actual_outcome, predicted_outcome, variance_score)

### Integration Points
- **Story 3.1**: Risk assessment integrated with patient profiles
- **Treatment System**: AI recommendations integrated with treatment planning
- **Appointment System**: Risk factors considered in scheduling decisions

### File Structure
- `lib/ai/risk-assessment.ts` - Core risk assessment algorithms
- `lib/ai/treatment-recommendations.ts` - Treatment recommendation engine
- `lib/ai/predictive-analytics.ts` - Predictive modeling system
- `components/ai/insights-dashboard/` - AI insights UI components
- `app/api/ai/patient-insights/` - Patient AI insights API endpoints
