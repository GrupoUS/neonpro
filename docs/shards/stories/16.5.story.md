## Dev Notes

### Technical Architecture Context
[Source: epic-16-advanced-ai-personalization.md] - **Research Integration**: Latest treatment protocols with evidence synthesis and ≥4.9/5.0 patient satisfaction target.

### Research Data Sources
- **PubMed/MEDLINE**: Biomedical literature and research articles
- **Cochrane Library**: Systematic reviews and evidence synthesis
- **ClinicalTrials.gov**: Clinical trial registry and study information
- **FDA Drug Database**: Approved treatments and safety information
- **Professional Guidelines**: Medical society recommendations and protocols

### Evidence Integration Process
1. **Literature Monitoring**: Automated search and filtering of new research
2. **Quality Assessment**: Evidence grading and bias detection
3. **Synthesis**: Meta-analysis and systematic review integration
4. **Translation**: Clinical guideline and protocol updates
5. **Implementation**: Care team notification and training
6. **Evaluation**: Outcome measurement and effectiveness tracking

### Research Analytics
- **Evidence Strength**: Grade of evidence and recommendation strength
- **Clinical Impact**: Effect size and clinical significance measures
- **Patient Outcomes**: Treatment effectiveness and safety profiles
- **Implementation Success**: Adoption rates and protocol compliance
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Economic analysis and value assessment

### Database Schema Extensions
- research_databases (id, database_name, api_endpoint, access_credentials, sync_frequency, last_sync)
- research_articles (id, pmid, title, authors, journal, publication_date, evidence_quality)
- clinical_trials (id, nct_id, trial_title, phase, enrollment_status, eligibility_criteria)
- evidence_recommendations (id, topic, recommendation_text, evidence_grade, guideline_source)
- research_integration (id, patient_id, research_finding_id, integration_date, clinical_impact)

### Integration Points
- **Story 16.1**: AI personalization with research-backed recommendations

- **Story 16.3**: Behavior prediction with research evidence
- **Story 16.4**: Treatment optimization with latest research findings
