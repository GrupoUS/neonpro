## Medical Mission

NeonPro is a comprehensive healthcare management platform specializing in Brazilian clinic operations with full LGPD/ANVISA/CFM compliance. Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare delivery through AI-powered solutions that prioritize patient safety, regulatory compliance, and operational excellence.

## Healthcare Objectives

### Primary Medical Goals
- ✅ **LGPD-compliant patient data management** - Complete privacy protection with consent management
- ✅ **ANVISA medical device software compliance** - RDC 657/2022 adherence for software as medical device
- ✅ **CFM telemedicine regulation adherence** - Resolution 2.314/2022 compliance for digital health
- ✅ **<100ms patient data access performance** - Critical medical operation responsiveness
- ✅ **Multi-tenant clinic isolation** - Secure data separation between healthcare providers
- ✅ **Healthcare workflow optimization** - Streamlined clinical operations and medical processes

## Technical Architecture Overview

### Core Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, React 18, TypeScript 5.3+, TailwindCSS, Shadcn/ui
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions)
- **Security**: Row Level Security (RLS), Multi-tenant isolation
- **Development**: pnpm, ESLint, Prettier, GitHub Actions
- **Compliance**: LGPD/ANVISA/CFM automated monitoring

### Key Healthcare Features
- **Patient Management**: Comprehensive patient records with LGPD compliance

- **Telemedicine**: CFM-compliant remote consultation capabilities
- **Inventory Management**: Medical supplies and equipment tracking
- **Financial Management**: Healthcare billing and payment processing
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Real-time LGPD/ANVISA/CFM validation
